## Business Concept

**Business Name:** Squared (styled as squared² or Squared²)

**Tagline:** The power of Square, squared

**Detailed Vision:** 
Squared is a specialized consultancy that unlocks the hidden potential of Square's API for Australian businesses. While Square provides an excellent POS system and basic online tools, most businesses only use 10% of what's possible through their API. We build beautiful, custom web experiences that leverage Square's free API tier to create storefronts, advanced inventory management, customer engagement tools, and business intelligence dashboards - all without subscription fees for basic Square users.

Our consultancy creates bespoke solutions for Square merchants - professional websites with integrated menus, product catalogs, online ordering, bookings, and customer loyalty programs - all powered by their existing Square data. We're not replacing Square - we're amplifying it. Each solution is tailored to the client's specific needs, whether that's a restaurant wanting online ordering, a retailer showcasing real-time inventory, or a service business accepting bookings.

What makes this special is that businesses can continue using Square's free tier while getting enterprise-level features through our custom implementations. The mathematical concept of "squared" perfectly captures this exponential value multiplication. As we grow and identify patterns, we may productize common solutions into a SaaS platform (Tier 2), but initially we're focused on high-value consulting engagements.

**Target Audience:**
- **Primary:** Established Australian SMBs using Square who need custom solutions (restaurants, multi-location retailers, service chains)
- **Secondary:** Growing businesses hitting Square's default limitations
- **Tertiary:** Web agencies needing Square API expertise for their clients
- **Pain Points:** Square's interface limitations, need for custom workflows, wanting integrated web presence, requiring business-specific features
